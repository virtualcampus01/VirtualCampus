Our History

Government Polytechnic Kotabagh was established in October 2020 by the Government of Uttarakhand to provide quality technical education in the Nainital region. The institute was created to meet the growing demand for skilled technical professionals in Uttarakhand.

Initially starting with two diploma programs, the institute has grown steadily while maintaining its commitment to excellence in technical education. Our campus in Kotabagh provides an ideal environment for learning with its peaceful surroundings and modern facilities.

Our Mission

To provide quality technical education that prepares students for successful careers in engineering and technology while developing them as responsible citizens.

  • To offer industry-relevant diploma programs
  • To develop technical and problem-solving skills
  • To foster innovation and creativity
  • To promote ethical values and social responsibility

Our Vision

To be recognized as a premier polytechnic institute that produces competent technical professionals capable of meeting global challenges and contributing to societal development.

Principal's Message

At Government Polytechnic Kotabagh, we employ rigorous and intensive integrated teaching methods to prepare our students for leadership positions in their chosen fields. Our institute emphasizes a combination of core technical subjects along with allied areas that contribute to the total personality development of our students.

Academic excellence, holistic education, global relevance coupled with contemporary curriculum form the educational foundation of our institution. I am confident these aspects will always make learning meaningful and rewarding for our students.

Department of Computer Science & Engineering

About the Department

The Department of Computer Science & Engineering at Government Polytechnic Kotabagh offers a 3-year diploma program that prepares students for careers in software development, networking, and emerging technologies.

Our curriculum is designed to provide hands-on experience with programming languages, database systems, web technologies, and computer networks. The department has well-equipped laboratories with modern computing facilities.

Program Highlights

  • Industry-relevant curriculum
  • Experienced faculty members
  • Modern computer labs
  • Project-based learning
  • Industry collaborations

Department of Electronics Engineering

About the Department

The Department of Electronics Engineering offers a comprehensive 3-year diploma program focusing on electronic circuits, embedded systems, communication technologies, and industrial automation.

Our students gain practical experience through laboratory work, projects, and industry visits. The department has well-equipped labs for analog and digital electronics, microcontrollers, and communication systems.

Program Highlights

  • Hands-on training with modern equipment
  • Focus on practical applications
  • Industry-standard labs
  • Workshops and seminars
  • Placement assistance

Admission Process

Government Polytechnic Kotabagh offers diploma programs in Computer Science & Engineering and Electronics Engineering. Admissions are conducted through the Uttarakhand Board of Technical Education admission process.

1

Check Eligibility

Candidate must have passed 10th standard with minimum 45% marks in Science and Mathematics.

2

Apply Online

Submit the online application form through the Uttarakhand Board of Technical Education portal.

3

Merit List

Admission is based on merit prepared from 10th standard marks.

4

Document Verification

Selected candidates must complete document verification and fee payment.

Important Dates

Application Start Date 1st June 2023
Last Date to Apply 30th June 2023
First Merit List 10th July 2023
Classes Begin 1st August 2023
